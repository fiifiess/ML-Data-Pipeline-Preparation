# Root Cause Analysis Framework

## Purpose

Provides a structured methodology for identifying the underlying causes of failures, defects, and process deviations.

## Scope

Applies to equipment breakdowns, quality escapes, safety incidents, and systemic operational issues.

## Trigger Conditions

RCA is initiated for:

* Recurrent incidents

* High-impact downtime

* Safety or compliance events

## Investigation Inputs

Analyses draw from:

* Machine and sensor logs

* Maintenance history

* Operator reports

* Production and quality data

## Analysis Techniques

Techniques include causal chain analysis, fault tree analysis, and contributing factor mapping.

## Outcomes and Tracking

Corrective and preventive actions are documented, assigned, and tracked to closure.

## Knowledge Capture

Completed analyses are retained for learning, prevention, and analytics use.
