# Equipment Decommissioning Process

## Purpose

Ensures equipment is retired in a controlled, safe, and environmentally responsible manner.

## Scope

Applies to machines, robots, auxiliary systems, and associated digital assets.

## Pre-Decommissioning

* Backup historical machine data and logs

* Identify hazardous materials or components

* Notify stakeholders and update asset registers

## Physical Decommissioning

Equipment is isolated from power, fluids, and networks before dismantling or removal.

## Data and System Updates

Decommissioned assets are removed from monitoring systems, maintenance schedules, and analytics pipelines.

## Environmental Considerations

Disposal or recycling follows environmental regulations and sustainability guidelines.

## Final Verification

Completion is documented with approvals and audit-ready records.
