# Supplier Quality Management

## Purpose

Establishes a structured approach for evaluating, monitoring, and improving supplier quality performance to ensure incoming materials meet manufacturing and regulatory requirements.

## Scope

Applies to all external suppliers providing raw materials, components, tooling, and outsourced services that influence product quality or production reliability.

## Supplier Qualification

Suppliers are evaluated based on:

* Process capability and certifications

* Historical quality performance

* Traceability practices

* Supply continuity risk

## Incoming Quality Control

Incoming materials undergo:

* Sampling-based inspections

* Dimensional and material verification

* Documentation and certification checks

## Performance Monitoring

Supplier scorecards track:

* Defect frequency and severity

* Delivery adherence

* Corrective action responsiveness

## Corrective Action Management

Nonconformances trigger formal root cause analysis and corrective action plans with defined verification steps.

## Strategic Feedback

Supplier quality trends inform sourcing strategy and risk management decisions.
