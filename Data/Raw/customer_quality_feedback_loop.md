# Customer Quality Feedback Loop

## Purpose

Ensures customer-reported quality issues are systematically analyzed and translated into corrective manufacturing actions.

## Scope

Covers external defect reports, warranty claims, and customer audits related to manufactured automotive parts.

## Intake and Classification

Customer feedback is logged and categorized by:

* Defect type

* Severity and safety impact

* Affected product and process step

## Correlation Analysis

External quality signals are correlated with:

* Internal inspection results

* Machine logs and process parameters

* Supplier material records

## Corrective Actions

Identified root causes trigger process adjustments, tooling changes, or supplier interventions.

## Feedback Closure

Resolution status is communicated back to customers and tracked for recurrence prevention.
