# Environmental Compliance Guidelines

## Purpose

Defines environmental management practices to ensure manufacturing operations comply with environmental regulations and internal sustainability goals.

## Scope

Covers emissions, waste handling, water usage, chemical management, and energy efficiency across the facility.

## Monitoring and Controls

Environmental performance is tracked via:

* Emissions monitoring systems

* Waste and recycling logs

* Energy and water consumption meters

## Operational Safeguards

Controls are implemented to:

* Prevent spills and leaks

* Reduce emissions during abnormal operations

* Ensure safe handling of hazardous materials

## Reporting and Audits

Environmental data is reviewed periodically and submitted to authorities as required. Internal audits validate compliance.

## Continuous Improvement

Incidents and deviations drive corrective actions and long-term environmental performance improvements.
