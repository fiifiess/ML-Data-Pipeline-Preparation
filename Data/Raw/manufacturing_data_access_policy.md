# Manufacturing Data Access Policy

## Purpose

Defines governance controls for accessing, modifying, and distributing manufacturing operational data.

## Scope

Applies to machine data, sensor streams, maintenance records, quality results, and analytics outputs.

## Access Principles

* Least-privilege access by default

* Role-based authorization

* Segregation between operational and analytical systems

## Role Definitions

Access levels are defined for operators, engineers, maintenance staff, analysts, and management.

## Authentication and Logging

All data access is authenticated and logged to support audits and incident investigations.

## Data Protection

Sensitive data is protected through encryption, network segmentation, and access reviews.

## Review and Enforcement

Access permissions are reviewed periodically and adjusted based on role changes or risk assessments.
