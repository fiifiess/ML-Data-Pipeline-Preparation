# Audit and Compliance Checklist

## Purpose

Provides a structured mechanism for verifying adherence to internal standards, regulatory obligations, and contractual requirements.

## Scope

Covers safety practices, maintenance records, data governance, environmental compliance, and training documentation.

## Checklist Structure

Audit items are grouped by domain:

* Safety and risk controls

* Equipment and maintenance documentation

* Data integrity and traceability

* Environmental and regulatory compliance

## Execution

Audits are conducted periodically by internal teams or external assessors. Evidence is collected, reviewed, and scored.

## Findings Management

Non-conformances are logged with severity ratings and corrective action plans.

## Data Linkage

Audit outcomes are linked to underlying operational records to ensure traceability.
